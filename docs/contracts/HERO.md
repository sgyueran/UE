# Component Contract: HeroSection

## Responsibility
Communicate professional identity in the first viewport.

## Required Content
- name
- role
- professional domains
- short summary
- projects CTA
- resume CTA
- contact CTA

## Rules
- Must not load Three.js.
- Background video must be optional.
- Must provide poster fallback.
- Must remain readable when media fails.
- Must not exceed first-load performance budget.
